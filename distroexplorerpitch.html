This shiny app allows the user to explore the properties of different probability distributions.

When first studying probability, students are presented with a number of different distributions which they may be unfamiliar with. This simple app allows them to explore the behaviour of seven common distributions.

The default settings will plot a simple normal distribution.

Distributions can also be plotted one on top of another.

Simultaneous plots can be of the same distribution or different distributions. The maximum number of simultaneous plots is three.
